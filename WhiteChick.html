Description

White Chicks (2004) is a comedy movie about two FBI agents who go undercover to protect two rich socialite sisters. When the real sisters refuse to go, the agents decide to disguise themselves as them instead. While pretending to be the girls, they get into many funny and awkward situations. Along the way, they learn about friendship, identity, and being true to themselves, making the movie both hilarious and entertaining.
