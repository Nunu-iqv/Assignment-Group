Description

Elemental (2023) is a heartwarming animated movie set in Element City, where people of fire, water, air, and earth live together. The story follows Ember, a strong and passionate fire girl, and Wade, a kind and emotional water boy, who come from very different worlds. When they meet, they slowly learn to understand each other and discover that even opposites can connect. Through their journey, the movie talks about love, family, and accepting differences in a beautiful and meaningful way.
